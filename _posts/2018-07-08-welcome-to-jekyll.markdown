Data has been gathered fro the sailboat Galatea, sail number 285, for the Single/Double race that was held in Bayfield WI on June 16, 2018.

The raw data, logged from the boat's NMEA 2000 bus, is availale for download from s3. This data can be converted to human readable form using canboat.
